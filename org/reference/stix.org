#+title: Structured Threat Information Expression

STIX is a language and serialization format used to exchange [[id:e33d50e1-cbac-4f84-b942-0439de3b41aa][cyber threat
intelligence]]. STIX is open-source.

STIX was created to simplify the sharing and consumption of CTI. STIX represents
all aspects of suspicion, compromise and attribution clearly with objects and
descriptive relationships.
