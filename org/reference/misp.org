#+title: MISP

MISP is an open-source platform for creating and sharing [[id:e33d50e1-cbac-4f84-b942-0439de3b41aa][cyber threat
intelligence]]. It features its own threat-intelligence format, and also supports
exporting to standard formats such as xml, or [[id:c6471d5d-59f8-44de-ae9a-2755acc7d171][STIX]].
