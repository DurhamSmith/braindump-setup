#+title: Ear Training
#+filetags: music

Ear training is to train the ear to be able to identify pitches, melodies,
chords, intervals, rhythms and other elements of music with their ear.

To train for relative pitch, one can do [[id:18a9e4a2-8da1-4fd1-80f9-9f0213bb9573][interval ear training]].
