1.  Mix
    -   51.2uL total
    -   0.2uL CNT
        -   **NOTE:**  It is extremely difficult to pippette this much, so exact number might be somewhat off
    -   50uL QD (raw and centrifuged)
    -   100mM NaCl (1uL)
2.  Anneal
    -   40C Hold for 9h
    -   40C &rarr; 23C at 0.1C/min
