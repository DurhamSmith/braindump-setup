## Protocols {#protocols}

1.  Mix
    -   50uL total
    -   2uL CNT
    -   10uL QD
    -   10mM Tris (0.5uL)
    -   10mM Mg (0.5uL)
    -   100mM NaCl (1uL)
    -   36 uL diH2O
2.  Anneal
    -   33C Hold for 9h
    -   33C &rarr; 23C at 0.1C/min
