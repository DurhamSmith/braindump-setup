## Protocols {#protocols}

1.  Mix
    -   51.5uL total
    -   0.5uL CNT
        -   **NOTE:**  It is extremely difficult to pippette this much, so exact number might be somewhat off
    -   40uL QD
    -   1M NaCl (10uL)
    -   HEPES 17mM
        -   .875uL
    -   6mM Mg2+
        -   0.3uL
2.  Anneal
    -   33C Hold for 9h
    -   33C &rarr; 23C at 0.1C/min
