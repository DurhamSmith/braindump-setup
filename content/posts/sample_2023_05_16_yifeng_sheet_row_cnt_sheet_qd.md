## Protocols {#protocols}

1.  Mix
    -   2nM sheet (3.1uL)
    -   5uL sheet-qd (supernatant and filtered)
    -   100mM NaCl 1uL
    -   10mM Mg 0.5uL
    -   10mM Tris 0.5uL
    -   diH2O 39.9uL
2.  Anneal
    -   33C Hold for 9h
    -   33C &rarr; 23C at 0.1C/min
