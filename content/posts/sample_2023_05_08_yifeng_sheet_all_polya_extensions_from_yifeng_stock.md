-   Used 100uL
-   Called **All-Exts Yifeng Stock 23/5/8**

-   Stock solution contained

    -   100nM Staple
    -   12.5mM Mg2+
    -   10mM Tris

    -   All 28 A<sub>21</sub> Extensions

-   Annealing:
    -   "Sheet 2h 30min" program
    -   95C hold for 2 minutes
    -   95C &rarr; 65C at -0.1C per 6 seconds
