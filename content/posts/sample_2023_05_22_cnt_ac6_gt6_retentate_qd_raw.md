-   5M NaCl
-   [Sample: 2023-05-22 Red CdTe Quantum Dots]({{< relref "sample_2023_05_22_red_cdte_quantum_dots.md" >}})

## Protocols {#protocols}

1.  Mix
    -   0.5uL CNTs
    -   200uL QDs
    -   NaCl bring concentration to 0, 0.1M, 0.4M and 1M
2.  Anneal
    -   33C Hold for 9h
    -   33C &rarr; 23C at 0.1C/min
